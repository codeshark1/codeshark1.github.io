Issues of travel insurance

Date: 19 марта 2015

Category: Russia

Author: John

According to the Russian Federation Federal Law No. 114-FZ of August 15, 1996 «On Procedure for Exit from the Russian Federation and Entry to the Russian Federation»

Entry to the Russian Federation for a foreigner or a stateless citizen is not permitted if the foreigner or stateless citizen does not have health insurance valid in the territory of the Russian Federation.
